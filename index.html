Geschwender is the leading manufacturer of offshore living accommodation furniture.

Global leaders in offshore accommodation furniture

The name Geschwender is known throughout the world, primary for our offshore cabin furniture. The strength, durability and ability to withstand fire and water ingress has set our product's special High Pressure Laminate Compact(HPL-Compact) material apart from all competitors, allowing us to provide our clients with IMO / NORSOK marine furniture that has the guarantee of 10 years and a life expectancy of 30-40 years.

No better example of our products strength is our Pullman bunk; completely self-supporting with no structural wall reinforcing required, allowing it to be installed in any new build or retro-fitted into any exesting cabin.

Our innovative swivel bunk / rotating bunk which resolves within its footprint revolutionised offshore accommodation design and immediately provided our clients with potential to increase their Person On Board (POB) numbers by 50%

Whilst we are well known for our cabin furniture, we also manufacture fitted furniture for all areas of offshore living quarters.

From catering, mess and tea point servery counters to cinema and TV entertainment units; all seamlessly integrating equipment and services for a completely considered and future-proofed end product, both aesthetically presented yet structurally built to outlast the lifespan of the platform.

The name Geschwender is also known throughout the world for our core product; our HPL Compact lockers.

Installed onshore throughout Europe and on offshore pplatforms globally, their strength, ease of cleaning and iconic aesthetic appearance make them the sole choice for most facilities looking to invest in an 'install-and-forget' solution - we've yet to experience a single door hinge failure! Available in any width and height combination, door shape and tier quantity - all within the same installation!

Every item manufactured by Geschwender is bespoke and made to order allowing our HPL-Compact fitted furniture to be adapted to support a wide range of other sectors:

Beauty and hair salons, care homes, dental and medical facilities, education facilities, food processing facilities, football stadiums, leisure yachts, museums, olympic training facilities, prison institution and rehabilitation centres, reception areas, spa and wellness retreats ans student accommodation.
